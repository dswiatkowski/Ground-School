KAPA — Centennial Airport

Englewood, Colorado. One of the busiest GA airports in the United States. Class D under Denver Class B shelf. High elevation, long runways, and the kind of summer afternoon density altitude that demands you respect every performance chart.

// Summer Hazards

Afternoon thunderstorms (peak 2-5 PM). Density altitude exceeding 9,000 ft on hot afternoons. Outflow boundaries causing sudden wind shifts. Be on the ground at destination by 2 PM.

// Winter Hazards

Crosswind on the long 17/35 runways. Icing in low overcast. Blowing snow reducing visibility. Cold, dense air gives great performance — but the inversion-trapped haze near the surface can cut viz to marginal VFR fast.
